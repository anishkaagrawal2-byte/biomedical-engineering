Introduction

Medical Imaging involves the visualization of internal structures of the body for diagnosis, monitoring, and treatment. It is one of the most crucial areas of biomedical engineering.

Main Imaging Techniques

  • Magnetic Resonance Imaging (MRI)
  • Computed Tomography (CT Scan)
  • Ultrasound Imaging
  • X-Ray Imaging
  • PET Scan

Applications

  • Diagnosis of tumors and cancers
  • Cardiovascular imaging
  • Neurological disorders
  • Orthopedic imaging
  • Guided surgeries and interventions

Recent Advances

Advances in imaging include 3D reconstruction, AI-based analysis, higher resolution MRI and CT, portable ultrasound devices, and fusion imaging.
